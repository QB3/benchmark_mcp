The objective is:
\begin{equation*}
    \min_{w \in \mathbb{R}^p}
        \frac{1}{2 n} \Vert y - X w \Vert^2
        + \sum_{j=1}^p \rho_{\lambda, \gamma}(w_j)
\end{equation*}

The penalty is:
\begin{equation*}
    \rho_{\lambda, \gamma}(t) =
    \begin{cases}
        \lambda |t| - \frac{t^2}{2 \gamma} \enspace, &\text{if } |t| < \lambda \gamma \enspace, \\
        \frac{\lambda^2 \gamma}{2} \enspace, &\mathrm{otherwise} \enspace.
    \end{cases}
\end{equation*}
